About the character

Burnice White — is part of the Sons of Calydon faction. The girl is described as a vibrant and friendly personality, capable of charming anyone, including monsters from the cavern. With incredible skill in creating various drinks, she works part-time as a bartender and is also responsible for all the nitro-fuel for the gang's mechanical equipment.

Rotation

Based on the nature of the effects triggered, White's main task in the heat of battle is to apply the Burn status and partially accumulate the fire anomaly on targets. After switching to the pocket, the next anomaly damage dealer begins filling their elemental gauge while simultaneously "finishing off" Burn with periodic damage from Burnice from the pocket. Thus, in the shortest possible time, two reactions causing Chaos occur one after another — all the damage from the first reaction, including any delayed effect due to its duration, stacks with the second attribute anomaly.

1. Depending on the team composition,Burnice can either initiate the battle or take the role of the 2nd or 3rd agent, stepping in during intervals to apply effects.. When the heroine appears on the field, accumulate energy for the charged EX ability. through basic attacks or by assisting in defense. You can enhance the used skill to "High-Temperature Mixing - Double Portion"by repeating the action.

2.After all the required targets are inflicted with Burn, switch heroes. Attack burning enemies to activate Charring, which will give bonus fire damage to the target and fill the "circle" of the fire anomaly.

3.Be sure to give Burnice time on the field, especially if you don't have a signature weapon. The heroine needs to accumulate and spend energy to replenish Heat points to deal bonus damage from pocket.

4.In Chaos teams or when there is a pressing need to replenish Heat, or to apply a Fire Anomaly to a target, use Burnice'sultimate. The rest of the time, save the ultimate for your main DPS and other allies.
